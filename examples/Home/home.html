Magic Circles

About

Magic Cirlces by Azureda is a library written with JavaScript designed to make circular patterns.

With Magic Circles, you have several advantages:

  • Repeatable, declarative markup
  • 100% SVG Graphics Compatible with most browsers
  • Full style customization with CSS
  • Circles animate in and out

What Can You Use Magic Circles For?

First of all, Magic Circles is licensed to you, for free, to modify and use in your commercial and non-commercial projects how you see fit.

Magic Circles make ideal decorations as well as innovative menus and preloaders.

Magic Circles are also printable directly out of the browser. You can use it to make custom posters or even a custom dartboard in minutes.

Usage ideas:

  • Meaningless decorations
  • Put your iPad on the floor and summon mighty demons
  • Fast, lightweight preloader
  • Wedding invitations
  • Attractive coasters
